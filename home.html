What is HowBusyIsMarino.com?

HowBusyIsMarino.com is a website summarizing Marino activity over previous periods of time that produces graphical predictions for future capacity at Marino. This allows users to plan their future workouts accordingly. We also have information about SquashBusters!



About Us

We are 4 Northeastern students who wanted to know when it is best to go to Marino. Most of the time, we could look at the Marino capacity info, but that was not efficient enough for us. We wanted to be able to plan out future workouts based on previous data. This website was our solution.
